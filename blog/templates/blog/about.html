About The Blog

The Flumoo blog is an extension of the dashboard site. It allows for user discussion on topics rendered from the data, as well as, information that may not neccesarily fit the Flumoo dashboard site. A wide array of topics are posted on the Flumoo blog. These include interpretations of the data, outside information relating to various firms or securities, and subjects like politics or economics that are relevant, yet outside the scope of the main Flumoo Dashboard site.

The dashboard is meant to serve as the main backbone of the website, while the blog is meant to serve as a forum for members to discuss the dasboard and add information that might be relevant, but not included in the dashboard.
